HaXmas 2022

For the 6th consecutive year, the Rotaract Club of IIT is back with Project Haxmas. Haxmas 2022 is set to be a combination of a hackathon and ideathon.

On the ideathon the competing teams are to pitch their new innovative ideas on the field of computing. They are to present their ideas in front of a panel of judges, then will be shortlisted under the development feasibility and other requirements.

Then the selected teams will be provided a month to work on their developments and implementations.

See more on our official RACIIT website

FACTS ABOUT HAXMAS22

Haxmas aims to provide one large platform for the innovative undergraduates of the nation, to showcase their implementations and creativity in the field of computing. It also aims to develop the professional and collaborative skills of the undergraduates, so that they are given with an insight of the world of innovative developments.

Ideathon

The competitor team which each consisted of 5-6 participants are to pitch their new innovative ideas according to the categories initialized as:

  • Data Science and Machine Learning
  • IT in Business and Finance
  • Game Development
  • IoT and Web Development
  • Artificial Intelligence

Knowledge Guide Series

A knowledge guide series consisting of series of seminars and webinars will be conducted under the categories given as:

  • Data science and machine learning
  • IT in Business and finance
  • Game development
